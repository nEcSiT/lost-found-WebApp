About Campus Lost & Found

Level 100 Computer Science Project - Accra Technical University

The Campus Lost & Found system was a project given to Computer Science students in Level 100 at Accra Technical University. As a team of software Computer Science students, we've come up with the innovative idea to build this lost and found system whereby students can report lost items or found items and get reunited with their belongings. Our system is designed to help students, faculty, and staff quickly report and recover lost items. Built with modern technology and a user-friendly interface, we're making campus life a little bit easier, one found item at a time.
